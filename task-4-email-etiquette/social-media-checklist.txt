RESPONSIBLE SOCIAL MEDIA USE CHECKLIST 

A quick guide to maintaining a positive, safe, and professional presence online.

------------------------------------------------------------

DO's

1. Think before you post
   Consider how your content may affect your reputation in the future.

2. Protect your privacy
   Use strong passwords and review your privacy settings regularly.

3. Share accurate information
   Verify facts before posting or sharing content.

4. Be respectful and professional
   Communicate politely and avoid unnecessary conflicts.

5. Use social media productively
   Build your network, showcase skills, and learn new things.

------------------------------------------------------------

DON'Ts

1. Don't share sensitive personal information
   Avoid posting details like your address, phone number, or financial info.

2. Don't post offensive or inappropriate content
   This includes hate speech, explicit material, or harmful jokes.

3. Don't engage in cyberbullying
   Never harass, threaten, or demean others online.

4. Don't spread misinformation
   Avoid forwarding rumors or unverified news.

5. Don't let social media affect your studies
   Maintain a healthy balance between online activity and academics.

------------------------------------------------------------

PRO TIP:
Always assume that anything you post online can become public and permanent.
